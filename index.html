CamNinja – Support

About the App

CamNinja is a fast-paced fruit slicing game powered by real-time hand tracking. Use your hands to slice fruit and build combos.

Contact Support

Troubleshooting

Camera not working:
Make sure camera permissions are enabled in System Settings.

Tracking issues:
Ensure your hands are clearly visible and lighting is good.

Compatibility

Supports macOS, iPhone, and iPad.

Privacy

CamNinja uses the camera only for gameplay. No images or data are recorded or stored.
