CamNinja – Privacy Policy

Overview

CamNinja respects your privacy. This app is designed to function without collecting or storing personal data.

Camera Usage

CamNinja uses your device’s camera for real-time hand tracking gameplay. Camera data is processed live and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Data Collection

CamNinja does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
